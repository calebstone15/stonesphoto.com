About Me

Hello, I'm Caleb and have been a photographer for almost a decade. I do many different types of photography from engagements to rocket launches. My goal is to give you a non traditional photo experience where you get the highest quality and lowest price photos. For me, this is a fun side gig that gets me away from my typical engineering mindset where I can relax and enjoy the art of photography.
