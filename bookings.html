Booking Process

Thank you for your interest in booking a photoshoot with me. Here's how the booking process works:

  • 1

    Submit Your Request

    Fill out the booking form with your preferred date, location, and type of photoshoot.

  • 2

    Confirmation

    I'll contact you within 24-48 hours to confirm availability and discuss the details of your shoot.

  • 3

    Deposit

    A 25% deposit is required to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due on the day of the shoot.

  • 4

    Photoshoot Day

    I'll meet you at the agreed location. We'll take as many photos as needed to capture your perfect moments.

  • 5

    Delivery

    You'll receive all photos within 2-3 days, with your 30 edited photos delivered within 7-10 days after selection.
