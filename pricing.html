Total Cost Formula

Total Cost = Travel Cost + Daily Rate + Optional Edits

Travel Cost is calculated as $0.20 per mile for round-trip travel. The Daily Rate is $200 per day, and optional edits are $2 per photo beyond the 30 free edits included.

This formula applies to all types of shoots, including cars, engagements, weddings, portraits, and more.

What You Get

  • 100% of the photos taken during the shoot, including raw files.
  • 30 free edited photos of your choice, with additional edits available for $2 per photo.
  • No watermarks, full ownership of all photos.
  • My time and dedication for as long as you need during the shoot, without any additional hourly charges.
  • 14 bit RAW 45mp photos. (If you dont know what this means it's very high resolution photos and easily editable files)

  • Daily Rate: $200

Why This Business Model Allows Me to Have Such Low Prices

Why am I so cheap you may ask? By focusing on just simply capturing the raw photos without having to edit immediately I am able to get you hundreds of photos the day of the shoot. All I have to do is just upload the photos. So you get more photos and faster delivery.

Another reason is this is just a side job for me. Many photographers try to make a career out of photography and reveal that with their pricing when in reality it is just meant to be a side job. I do this because it is relaxing to me not to make a living.
